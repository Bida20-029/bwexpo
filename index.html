About the Food Expo

The Food Expo is Botswana’s first large-scale expo dedicated to transforming the food sector through innovation, enterprise, and collaboration.

The event aims to:

  • Promote local production and reduce food imports
  • Catalyze value chain growth
  • Build resilience through sustainable food systems
  • Support youth, women, and food-entrepreneurs
